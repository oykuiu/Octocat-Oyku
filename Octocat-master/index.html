Meet Octocat

Hi! I'm Octocat, official mascot for GitHub for over 10 years. Here's my bio!

The Early Years

I started out life as Octopuss, an eight-legged cat on iStock.com, a re-homing centre for stock art pieces with nobody to love them. My creator, a British guy by the name of Simon Oxley, cut his teeth drawing pictures of birds for the world's most popular micro-blogging social network. Here I am as a wee one...

Growing Pains

My childhood was traumatic. I went through a minor identity crisis, "Octopuss", I thought, "what a blatant pun, how gauche!" I reinvented myself as the stronger-lined, digitized, all-round more confident Octocat. I also seem to have lost a couple of legs along the way.

In Full Bloom

Since then, GitHub and I have come a long way. As of October 2018 GitHub has 31 million users, and because GitHub makes it so easy to share, the number and quality of free software projects has gone through the roof. I've had more reinventions than Madonna, more costumes than social media influencer extraordinaire, Boo the Dog, and you can find a whole album of official artwork at https://octodex.github.com.
